Overview

Hospital Institute Medical Sciences Private Limited is set to be the largest tertiary care private hospital of our Country having a wide range of advanced and sophisticated facilities and the latest Medicare technologies. Spread over three acres of land, situated at a strategic and futuristic location on the State highway near the coastline of the Arabian sea giving a breath-taking view of the beach and the stunning sky and with the state-of-art- facility, the promoters propose to offer world-class healthcare services to people of all socioeconomic statuses. It aims at elevating the Hospital Institute of Medical Sciences to an advanced research center, owing to the significance of education and research in the healthcare industry. The Hospital is facilitating the ongoing pursuit of excellence by bringing in not only the best clinical and administrative talent but also the ultra-modern equipment and technology enabling delivery of the highest standards of healthcare in India. The hospital has been the result of several years of substantial hard work, determination and perseverance with a vision to be excellence in healthcare by providing patient-centric, compassionate and affordable care with an internationally accepted sustainable outcome. It is confident that this hospital will create the energy and excitement that will attract the best medical faculty and staff for decades to come.
